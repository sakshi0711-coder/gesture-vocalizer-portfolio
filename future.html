Future Scope

Several enhancements are planned to broaden impact and functionality.

Custom Gesture App

Dedicated mobile application where users define their own gestures and shortcuts for personalized communication.

Personal Voice Cloning

Users can record their own voice to be used as the gesture output, making communication feel more personal and natural.

AI Voice Assistants

Integration with Alexa or Google Assistant embedded in the glove for smart home control via gestures.

Home Automation

Gesture shortcuts to turn lights on/off, adjust thermostat, or control IoT devices without any spoken word.

Indian Sign Language (ISL)

Expand the gesture vocabulary to support full ISL — both hands, broader vocabulary, and regional variations.

Machine Learning Integration

Train a model on user-specific gestures to improve accuracy and reduce false-positive recognition rates.
