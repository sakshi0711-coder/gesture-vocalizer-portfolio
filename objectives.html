Project Objectives

This project was built around five clear, measurable goals to ensure the device delivers real-world value.

Sign language is the only method and way in the communication between the normal person and dumb person, but most of normal people can not understand sign language because they do not know anything about sign language. Due to this disable person find very difficulties in their life. So, the main objective of our project is to make a very cost-effective system which will help dumb and deaf people to communicate with other. So, our project, smart glove will translate different hand gestures into sounds and convey the messages of disable person to others. So, by using smart glove there will be no communication barrier between the two communities and also this is our main objective of making of this project to help people. This project will remove the communication barrier between the disable person with the normal person. With the help of "Vocalizer" the deaf and dumb person's life will be much easier and they will get a chance to express themselves more and can get more opportunities in their life.

01

Convert hand gestures into accurate text and speech output

02

Enable easy, real-time communication for specially-abled individuals

03

Design a low-cost and user-friendly wearable device

04

Support a wide vocabulary of gestures, numbers and words

05

Make the system portable — no internet or mobile required

06

Display corresponding text messages on LCD for better communication in noisy areas.

07

Generate real-time voice output using the DF mini player.

08

Promote inclusive communication between deaf/mute and normal individuals.
