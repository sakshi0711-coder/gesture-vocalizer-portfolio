Problem Statement

Deaf and mute people rely on sign language, but most people don’t understand it. This creates a communication gap in daily life.

  • No common communication method
  • Dependency on others
  • Difficulty in emergency situations
  • Existing systems are costly

In the last few years, there has been exponential increase in the population of hearing impaired and speech disable people due to oral disease, birth defects or accident. When a disable person like deaf and dumb person speaks to a normal people then normal people find difficulty to communicate with the dumb and deaf people disable person like deaf and dumb faces many difficulties in their life. One of the main problems they are facing is about academic and social needs. Due to their disability the deaf and dumb person are ignored by the people and give less priority as compare to normal person. One of the main reasons why deaf and dumb people are being ignored is communication. Because most of the normal people cannot understand sign language. Due to this there is always a communication barrier between deaf and dumb people with the people.
