Conclusion:

The Gesture Vocalizer for Deaf and Mute project provides a simple and effective solution to bridge the communication gap between speech-impaired individuals and others. Using flex sensors and an accelerometer, the system converts hand gestures into real-time text and speech output. This project proves that a low-cost, portable, and standalone device can enable easy communication without the need for internet or smartphones. Overall, it highlights both technical innovation and social impact, with strong potential for future improvements and real-world use.
